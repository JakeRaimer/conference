Kristy Tillman

Panelist

Kristy Tillman is a designer living in San Francisco, California. She is currently the Head of Communication Design at Slack. She has also spent time on a volunteer basis with Code2040 on the Curriculum Steering Committee and she runs a weekly newsletter Tomorrow Looks Bright.
